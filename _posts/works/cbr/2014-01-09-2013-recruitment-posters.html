Recruitment Posters



Background
The Cornell Business Review is a magazine organization that strives to encourage the education and discussion of business trends and issues among the Cornell student body by publishing a business review and hosting related events.

Recruitment Project
In a bigger than ever effort to recruit talented freshmen for our magazine, I redesigned CBR's old cramped, clipart feeling posters into attention-grabbing, clean-edged headlines.
