Background About Ellaspark
"If you have no connections to rich friends or venture capitalists to back you, you’ll likely be faced with the difficult decision of risking it all like we did and/or have to turn ramen into a staple part of your diet (yum!). This isn’t necessary a bad way to start. But we believe ellaspark will provide a better way to the early funding needs of startups through easier to access non-dilutive capital plus the opportunity to get valuable feedback from the community. As a result, our community will be empowering more amazing startups to get off the ground and succeed."
