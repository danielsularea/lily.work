— Welcome

I'm Lily, a Cornell University senior finishing my computer science major in chilly Ithaca, NY. I was born in China with my artist grandfather and raised in America by my programmer father. As a result, I have a great appreciation for art and technology and everything in between. During my years at Cornell, I spent more time with startups (about a dozen or so) than in class, wherein the former sometimes schooled me pretty hard anyways. After graduation, I will be moving on to be a software designer for IBM at the Cambridge office, where I hope to get paid to do more cool stuff.

I believe in minimaism and I love experiments. Right now, I'm going through a phase of applying physical design to digital design, particularly print and packaging. I also like to do new things for the heck of it, no matter how obviously useless or ironically useful it is. Recent "for-kicks" achievements include: snowboarding, boxing, Arduino, and ink art. My biggest fear is running out of time to do all the side projects I keep piling up on my notepad.

Check out my blog or Twitter to see anything else I'm up to. While I'm not currently exploring new opportunities, I would love to talk about your next big idea so give me a shout.

P.S. This TED talk changed my life.
