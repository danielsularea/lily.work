Title

Björn Rühmann first found success as a creative at TBWA Paris, working on a series of award-winning international campaigns. He launched his directing career in 2007 as co-founder of lauded directing collective The Vikings, whose work attracted over 100 international awards. With films such as the universally beloved 'Power of Wind' for energy company Epuron, which was the second most awarded commercial worldwide in 2007 and garnered top honors at Cannes, Björn ventured out on his own as a director.
