Rebranding to appeal to the fast paced and modern customers
original logo
new logo

First draft saw a change of font and color schemes but kept a familiarity to the original logo.

Then I decided to move away from familiarity because the feeling it gave was corporate and constricting. This was undesirable to our target audience and does not offer the highly innovative and whimsical ambience that Fiberspark actually embodies. I changed the font to embrace a more modern look and played with a hummingbird to convey speed.

Finally, I finalized the hummingbird into something more "high speed internet" and less "boutique" by playing with its form and lines.
